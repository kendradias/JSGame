How to Play

Objective

Collect all white pellets on the board while avoiding the enemies. Once you've collected all pellets, you win!

Controls

Use these keys to navigate through the maze:

  • 'W' - Move Up
  • 'A' - Move Left
  • 'S' - Move Down
  • 'D' - Move Right

Game Features

  • Enemies will chase you through the maze
  • Choose your difficulty level to adjust enemy speed and count
  • Use the Play/Pause button to pause the game at any time
  • Reset button will restart your current game
  • End Game button will end your current game
